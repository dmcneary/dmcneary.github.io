About Me

I grew up in Klamath Falls, Oregon and spent my first two years of life in a house across from the rich kids' school. I don't remember much of it. I mostly remember Big White House - the corner duplex my parents had purchased for $10,000, my mother clocking off graveyard from the hospital as we were going off to school, my dad renovating the house into the night after his shift at the hardware store. I remember the Catholic school across the street which my dad went to as a kid, and the earthquake in '93 that destroyed it, and the Methodists who bought the leftover playground on the next lot & told us we couldn't play there anymore.

I'm a photographer by art, bicycle mechanic by trade, musician by heart, and web developer in training. Please check out my media profiles below.
